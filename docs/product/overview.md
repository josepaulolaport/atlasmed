# Product Overview

## Vision

Atlasmed is an AI-powered healthcare relationship and intelligence platform that connects pharmaceutical and healthcare commercial teams, clinics, physicians, and partner organizations through CRM, territory management, healthcare data, workflow automation, analytics, and an AI assistant that can answer questions and take governed actions.

## Target Users

- Pharmaceutical sales representatives.
- Sales managers.
- Regional managers.
- Healthcare commercial operations teams.
- Clinics and medical practices.
- Physicians.
- Healthcare organizations and partners.
- Future enterprise customers in the healthtech ecosystem.

## Core Platform Capabilities

- Multi-tenant organization management.
- Identity, RBAC, MFA, SSO readiness, and audit logging.
- Healthcare CRM for clinics, physicians, relationships, visits, notes, and follow-ups.
- Territory management and clinic assignment workflows.
- Workflow automation for tasks, reminders, approvals, and follow-up actions.
- Notifications through in-app, email, push, calendar reminders, and task alerts.
- Web dashboards and analytics for performance, coverage, activity, and usage.
- Mobile field workflows for representatives.
- Admin dashboard for users, permissions, territories, integrations, audit, monitoring, and AI governance.
- AI assistant for permission-aware questions and actions across the platform.

## Product Principles

- Healthcare data access must be least-privilege, auditable, and tenant-scoped.
- Field workflows must be fast, simple, and mobile-first.
- Managers need analytics they can trust and trace back to operational data.
- AI-generated actions must be permission-aware, logged, and reviewable.
- The platform should evolve from a modular monolith before considering service decomposition.
